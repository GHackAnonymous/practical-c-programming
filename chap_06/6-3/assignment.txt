Exercise 6-3

Modify exercise 6-2 to print a + or - after the letter grade, based
on the last digit of the score. Modifiers are:

last digit  modifier
----------  --------
 1-3         -
 4-7         <blank>
 8-0         +

For example, 81=B-, 94=A, and 68=D+. An F is only an F. There is
no F+ or F-.
