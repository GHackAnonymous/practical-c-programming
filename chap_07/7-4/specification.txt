Salestax

Specification

Dan Lowe
13 Jan 2011

Salestax is a program that will add an 8% sales tax to a given amount,
then round the result to the nearest penny.

If '0' is entered, the program ends.

Example:

    Enter amount or '0' to quit: 4.35
    After tax, amount is $4.70

    Enter amount or '0' to quit: 94.38
    After tax, amount is $101.93
