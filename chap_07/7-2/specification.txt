Datemath
Preliminary specification
10 Jan 2011
Dan Lowe

Datemath is a program that calculates the number of days between two
dates. It understands leap years and accounts for them.

Example:

    Enter a starting date (month day year): 1 15 1991
    Enter an ending date (month day year): 2 28 1991
    1/15/1991 - 2/28/1991 = 45 days (inclusive)
