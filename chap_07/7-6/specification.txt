PosNeg

Specification

14 Jan 2011
Dan Lowe

PosNeg takes a set of numbers in and counts how many are positive and how many
are negative. If '0' is entered, the program ends and the counters are
displayed.

Example:

	Enter '0' to end the program.

	Enter a value: 4
	Enter a value: 9
	Enter a value: -3
	Enter a value: 46
	Enter a value: 39
	Enter a value: -9
	Enter a value: 0

	Counted 4 positive, 2 negative values.
